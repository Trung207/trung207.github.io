STAGE OF WORK

1 Briefing

We start our work with an acquantaince. We discuss

the project and define the goals of the future site.

You submit all the content you have for the project. I

make a work plan and we agree all time of

completion of the work

2 Analysing

I collect nesceccery information about the business,

analyze competitors. then I look refernces for a

future project and decide on the style.

3 Brainsorm and Moodboard

Moodboard is a collection of images and different

materials to visualize ideas and emotions that the design will bring.

It helps customers visualize design ideas and concepts

4 Sketch

Rough sketches can be done by hand or digitally.

Use the moodboard made in step 3 for inspiration

5 Perform

Send a demo to the customer to see if the design is

working as expected

6 Final design

After finish design, edit up to 3 times.
